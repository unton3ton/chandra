## West Germanic changes

- Loss of word final -z
- Apocope of final short vowels
- Monophthongization of unstressed diphthongs.

## Early Old English

- Shortening of unstressed long vowels.
- Fronting of -a to -æ

## Old English

- Merger of all surviving unstressed vowels to a/e/u (earlier -æ from fronted -a merges with -e)

## Late Old English

- Merger of all unstressed vowels to /a/.
- Merger of unstressed m/n
